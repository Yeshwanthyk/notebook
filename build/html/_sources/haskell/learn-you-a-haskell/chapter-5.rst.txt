---------
Recursion
---------

* In haskell, recursion is important because we do computations by declaring what something is instead if declaring how you get it.
* In recursive functions we should always identify the edge case and try to count down to that.
* All the examples can be found at `recursive fns`_

.. _recursive fns: https://github.com/Yeshwanthyk/gnana/blob/master/haskell/learn-you-a-haskell/recursive-fns.hs
